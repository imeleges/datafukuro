Brief description

This project takes a deep dive into the apartment listings of St. Petersburg using data from Yandex.Real Estate service. It aims to shed light on real estate market trends and property pricing.

Description

Utilizing a large dataset from Yandex.Real Estate service, this project provides an in-depth exploration of apartment listings in St. Petersburg, Russia.

The dataset of 23,699 rows, 22 columns and had some missing values, which required cleaning and processing for accuracy. Through this process, a few thousand rows that were less useful were eliminated, such as records with missing data for key fields like living area, kitchen area, and locality name.

With this refined dataset, I calculated critical parameters, such as price per square meter, and categorized properties by the floor type. Also extracted data related to the day of the week, month, and year a property was listed. This information helped identify market trends and key factors influencing property prices.

Further analysis revealed key determinants of property pricing, such as the property's total area and its proximity to the city center. This understanding of St. Petersburg's real estate market can be a valuable for prospective buyers, real estate professionals, and help to determine anomalies that might indicate fraud.
